Our Services

Safe, reliable transportation for dogs of all breeds and sizes.

Whether it's a routine vet appointment or a relocation across the city, Pet Passenger provides calm, insured, GPS-tracked transport tailored to your dog's needs. We serve individual pet owners and trade clients including breeders, kennels, and veterinary practices.

Vet & Medical Appointments

Safe, punctual transport to and from veterinary practices, specialist clinics, and animal hospitals. Ideal for owners who cannot drive or have anxious dogs that travel better with a calm, experienced handler.

Grooming & Daycare Transport

Door-to-door collection and drop-off for grooming salons, doggy daycare, and training classes. We work around your appointment times so your dog arrives relaxed and on schedule.

Airport & Travel Runs

Timely, stress-free transport to airports, train stations, and travel hubs when your itinerary requires your dog to be somewhere specific at a specific time.

Breeder & Kennel Transport

Reliable, professional collection and delivery for breeders, boarding kennels, and vet practices. We handle multiple dogs on scheduled routes — contact us to discuss regular trade bookings and rates.

Dog Relocation

Moving home? We'll transport your dog safely while you manage the move. Perfect for longer local journeys where a calm, focused ride matters most.
